The Process

How we made it happen.

01
📞
Reached Out

We contacted local schools and businesses to find the right partner, landing on Northview Elementary as our home base.

02
📣
Spread the Word

We made flyers, posted on Instagram, and hung signs all around Ball State campus to rally donations from Muncie.

03
🤝
Divided Tasks

Team members visited Northview to understand the space. Others organized logistics, sorted items, and bought supplies and detergent.

04
✨
Sorted & Prepared

We carefully sorted clothing by type and size, and gathered toiletries, shoes, and school supplies to include.

05
🏫
Delivered

Everything was transported to Northview Elementary and placed in the nurse's office — ready for students and families whenever they need it.

Our Mission

"To provide elementary students with adequate and appropriate clothing so that every child has what they need to feel confident and comfortable at school."

Our Closet was born from a simple observation: students in Muncie needed more than academic support — they needed clothing, shoes, and everyday essentials to show up ready to learn.

We partnered with Northview Elementary School, setting up a closet in the nurse's office stocked with donations from Ball State campus and the broader Muncie community.

We also connected with BSU's Cardinal Closet, learning from their experience to build something that could last beyond our program — a resource the school and students can continue using.
