The Process

Simple as 1, 2, 3.

Drop off gently used clothing at any of our collection points around Muncie. We accept all sizes — especially children's and adult clothing in good condition.

02

Our team carefully sorts every donation by size and type, ensuring each item is clean and ready for a new home. Every piece is treated with care.

Our Mission

"Every child deserves to walk into school with confidence — and the right clothes help make that happen."

Our Closet is a student-led initiative through the Muncie Leadership Program, committed to reducing clothing insecurity among elementary-aged children in the Muncie community.

We believe small acts of generosity — like donating a jacket or a pair of shoes — can have a lasting impact on a child's education, self-esteem, and sense of belonging.

All donations go directly to students at Muncie elementary schools, making sure nothing goes to waste and every item finds a family that truly needs it.
